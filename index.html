About Kathekani FC

Founded in 2008, our achievements include winning the Hon. Kalia Cup in 2010, 2013 Dr. Musembi Cup (Songeni), and consecutive victories in the Ligi Mashinani in 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020. Additionally, we claimed victory in the Muting'a Cup in 2018, the Katumo Cup in 2020, and the Darius Cup in 2022.
